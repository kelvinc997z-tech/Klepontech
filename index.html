Where the name comes from

Every address on the Ethereum blockchain starts with 0x. It's the prefix that identifies you on what is becoming the most important financial network being built today.

The 100x comes from the 100x Group, the company that created the perpetual futures product. Perpetual futures are arguably the second most important innovation in all of crypto behind stablecoins, and they are entirely unique to this asset class.
